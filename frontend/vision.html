Parkinson’s Telemonitoring

Our project aspires to evolve from early-stage detection to continuous telemonitoring of Parkinson’s patients. This means tracking subtle changes in voice, motor skills, and cognition remotely — helping clinicians adapt treatment in real-time.

Becoming a SaaS Platform

We envision building a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform dedicated to neuro research. Researchers worldwide will be able to plug in data, run analytics, and explore biomarkers for Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s, epilepsy, and other neurological disorders — all on one secure, scalable cloud-based platform.

Why This Matters

Current diagnostics are slow and fragmented. Our long-term vision is to unify clinical neuroscience tools, accelerate discovery, and improve patient quality of life through accessible, AI-driven insights.
